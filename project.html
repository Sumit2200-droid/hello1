About Project

Project Page of Ujjwal Kids World

Welcome to the Project Page of Ujjwal Kids World, where we showcase the diverse and innovative projects undertaken by our talented students. Our project-based learning approach allows students to explore their interests, apply their knowledge, and develop critical skills through hands-on experiences.

Students engage in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics projects, from building simple machines to creating art with technology. These projects encourage interdisciplinary learning and creativity.



Our students actively participate in community service projects, promoting social responsibility and empathy. These initiatives range from environmental conservation efforts to supporting local charities.



Through cultural projects, students explore and present on various traditions, festivals, and global cultures. These projects broaden their understanding of the world and promote cultural awareness.



Annual science fairs at Ujjwal Kids World allow students to showcase their scientific investigations and experiments, fostering a deep understanding of scientific principles and methods.

Projects

Our Projects

Automatic Night Light Sensor Project
At Prodigy Tech School, we are developing an Automatic Night Light Sensor Project, aimed at creating a responsive and energy-efficient lighting solution based on ambient light levels.
Obstacles Avoidance System
At Prodigy Tech School, our students are actively engaged in the development of an advanced Object Detection Project, leveraging state-of-the-art algorithms to accurately identify and classify objects in real-time scenarios.
Automatic Obstacles Avoidance Robot
At Prodigy Tech School, we're developing an Automatic Obstacle Avoidance Robot, integrating sophisticated sensors and algorithms to navigate environments autonomously while avoiding obstacles in its path.
Rifd Attendence
At Ujjwal Kids World, our students have successfully designed and implemented an RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) project, demonstrating their skills in technology and innovation. This project is a testament to our commitment to providing hands-on learning experiences that prepare students for the future.
Toy Train
At Ujjwal Kids World, our students have creatively designed and constructed a functional toy train that can accommodate 30 passengers, showcasing their ingenuity and practical skills. This project not only reflects their technical abilities but also their teamwork and dedication.
Led Blinking Light
At Ujjwal Kids World, our students have successfully completed an LED blinking project, showcasing their understanding of basic electronics and programming. This project is a fundamental part of our curriculum, designed to introduce students to the principles of circuitry and coding in a hands-on manner.
Non-Stop Lavalamp
At Ujjwal Kids World, our students have creatively designed and constructed a non-stop lava lamp, blending science, art, and engineering in an engaging and visually captivating project. This initiative showcases their innovative thinking and practical application of scientific principles.
Multiplayer Shooting Game
At Ujjwal Kids World, our students have showcased their programming skills and creativity by developing an exciting multiplayer shooting game using Scratch. This project is a testament to their ability to combine technical knowledge with imaginative game design.
Racing Car
At Ujjwal Kids World, our students have embarked on an exciting journey by designing and building a racing car, showcasing their skills in engineering, design, and teamwork. This project highlights their ability to apply theoretical knowledge to create a functional and engaging model.
